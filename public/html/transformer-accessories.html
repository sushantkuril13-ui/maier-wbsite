Transformer Accessories

We produce radiator valves and accessories for power transformers as well as hermetically sealed distribution transformers and reactors.
It began 65 years ago with radiator valves - nowadays we count dehydrating breathers, pressure relief devices, oil drain devices up to transformer protection relays such as the Buchholz relay MBP to the range of our products.
With our recently established transformer protection devices, we offer you and your customers the best possible protection at the highest quality level!
